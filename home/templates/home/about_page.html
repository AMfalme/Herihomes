Our Model

How we do this

The company prides itself on giving the client the value-added extras to make their experience one of a life time. This is sort of advice that can only come from many years of personal experience in the Hospitality, travel and Tourism sectors.

1
Reduced Cost

We reduce the cumulative cost of a housing unit thus eliminating the hefty interest that is ordinarily transferred to the end buyer by developers in where a debt model is employed. Stays in Mombasa has experienced phenomenal success mostly attributable to this unique housing model.

2
Affordability

The company also ensures that it constructs quality and affordable housing units in attractive locations that have high rental yield. In addition to real estate investments, the company has also invested portions of its assets in the purchase of land in strategic areas for resale.

3
Vision

We are currently developing housing units along Kikuyu road(3 Projects), Kitisuru (2 projects) and Athi River (Daystar)(1 project) using the equity model as described above. The rest of the capital is raised through presales of housing units.
